Fluffy Waffles

Make the fluffiest waffles you have ever made with this easy to make recipe. Add your favorite fruit toppings and a little bit (okay, maybe not so little) whipped cream to enjoy a great breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2)
  • All-purpose flour (2 cups)
  • Vegetable oil (1/2 cup)
  • White sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Baking powder (4 tsp)
  • Salt (1/4 tsp)
  • Vanilla extract (1/2 tsp)

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl with a whisk until they are fluffy.
  3. Next, add in the flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla. Whisk until contents are smooth.
  4. Spray the waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour the mixture onto the waffle iron.
  5. Cook waffles until golden brown (about four minutes).
  6. Serve hot and top with additional toppings if desired.
