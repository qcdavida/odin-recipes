French Onion Soup

A simple French onion soup

This simple recipe will have you thinking you are on the streets of Paris. Simple, yet rich, this recipe will make your mouth water.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter (1/2 cup)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Onions, sliced (4 cups)
  • Beef broth (4 cups)
  • Dry sherry (2 tbsp)
  • Dried thyme (1 tsp)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • French bread
  • Provolone cheese (4 slices or more)
  • Swiss cheese, diced (2 slices)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (1/4 cup)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with the olive oil in a stock pot (8 quart works best) over meduim heat.
  2. Add onions and stir until onions are translucent. Make sure NOT to brown the onions.
  3. Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Set your oven to broiler (high).
  5. Get a cooking sheet. Once the 30 minutes are up, fill some bowls up with the soup contents. Place on top of each bowl a slice of bread topped with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice of Swiss, and 1 tablespoon of Parmesan cheese.
  6. Place each bowl on the cooking sheet and place in oven and leave it until the cheese bubbles and is slightly brown.
