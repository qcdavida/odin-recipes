Homemade Chili

Heart warming bowl of chili

What is better than a bowl of chili on a cold, winter day? Nothing! Make this delicious chili recipe that will make you the envy of everyone on your block.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • White onion chopped (1)
  • Garlic colves minced (2)
  • Ground beef (2 lbs)
  • Kidney beans, rinsed and drained (2 16 oz. cans)
  • Diced tomatoes (1 28 oz. can)
  • Tomato puree (1 15 oz. can)
  • Water (1 cup)
  • Green chile peppers (1 4 oz. can)
  • Chili powder (2 tbsp or to taste)
  • Salt (2 tsp)
  • Ground cumin (2 tsp)
  • Ground black pepper (1 tsp)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add onion and garlic. Cook and stir for about five minutes.
  3. Add ground beef. Break up ground beef and cook until browned. About 8-10 minutes.
  4. Transfer beef mixture to instant pot. Stir in the rest of the ingredients.
  5. Set instant pot to 35 minutes and let it naturally release.
  6. Once done, serve in a bowl and top with shredded cheese.
